Calmora – Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Introduction

Calmora (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, and profile details you provide when creating an account.
  • Wellness Data: Mood logs, habit tracking, and journal entries you input in the app.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about app usage to improve performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app features like tracking habits, moods, and journals.
  • To personalize your experience and insights.
  • To improve and troubleshoot the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited data may be shared with trusted service providers (such as Firebase for authentication and database hosting) solely to support app functionality.

Data Retention & Deletion

You may request account deletion at any time via the app or our Account Deletion Page. When deleted, your personal data and wellness logs are permanently erased. Certain data may be retained for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, or legal purposes.

Children’s Privacy

Calmora is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards (such as Firebase security rules and encryption in transit) to protect your data.
