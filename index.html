Calmora – Account & Data Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your Calmora account and associated data:

  1. Open the Calmora app → go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
  2. Confirm your request in-app.
  3. Alternatively, you may submit a request using the button below.
Request Account Deletion

What Happens Next

  • Your account details, mood logs, journal entries, and habit data will be permanently deleted.
  • Certain data may be retained for up to 30 days for fraud prevention, legal, or security purposes.

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@calmora.app.
